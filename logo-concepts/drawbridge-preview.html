DrawBridge — Logo Evolution

Current vs D-Gate concepts

Current

Orange square, black trapezoid. The drawbridge dropping in perspective.

A — D-Arch Gate

D-shaped arch frames the gate. Trapezoid drawbridge drops inside. Orange on orange for the bridge.

B — Subtle Bridge

Same D-gate. Bridge is a slightly lighter dark — you can see it dropping but the gate dominates.

C — D-Frame on Dark

Dark background, orange D-frame is the gate. Bridge drops in orange. Inverted treatment.

Refined — tighter D shapes

D — Architectural D

Asymmetric D: straight left wall (the castle wall), arched right. More literal gate shape.

E — Fortress Gate

Thick gate walls + arch. Looking through. Bridge mid-drop. More complex, more castle.

F — D Outline + Bridge

Just the D letterform as a thick outline. Bridge fills the bottom. Most minimal, most "logo."
