DrawBridge — V2 & V4 Variations

V2 base (medium weight) — four treatments

V2a — Solid Silhouette

D and bridge as one filled shape. Bold, monolithic. The gate IS the mark.

V2b — Counter D

Filled D with orange counter. Bridge sits inside. Reads as a proper letter D with depth.

V2c — Double Line

Two parallel D strokes. Engineering/blueprint precision. Refined.

V2d — Ghost Frame

D fades back, bridge is solid. The gate is there but the bridge commands attention.

V4 base (heavy weight) — four treatments

V4a — Split Gate

Gap between D-arch and bridge. Suggests the hinge point where bridge separates from gate.

V4b — Bridge Window

Solid D, bridge cut out as negative space. You're looking through the gate at the lowering bridge.

V4c — Rounded Heavy

Heaviest stroke, rounded ends. Approachable, solid, slightly softer.

V4d — Depth

Gradient fill + subtle inner highlight. Dimensional, premium feel.
